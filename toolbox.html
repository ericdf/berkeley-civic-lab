Program Effectiveness and Cost-Effectiveness Data

We know where the money goes. What we don't have is systematic data on whether funded programs are working — outcome measures, cost-per-result comparisons, or independent evaluations of city-funded agencies.

This is a significant gap. The framework's third test — does it work, and how would we know? — cannot be answered without it.

This data does not currently exist in a public, accessible form. It should.
