Add Your Name

Endorse the Berkeley Budget Discipline Framework and support a better decision-making process for Berkeley's public dollars.

What you are endorsing: By adding your name, you are supporting the decision-making framework — not every future application of it. Different people may reasonably reach different conclusions when applying these principles to individual budget decisions.

Endorsing this framework means you believe every public dollar should be held to a consistent, transparent standard. It does not commit you to any specific budget outcome.

Six Questions Every Dollar Must Answer

  1. Is this a core city responsibility?
  2. Is it more important than competing needs?
  3. Does it work — and how would we know?
  4. Does outside funding actually reduce our burden?
  5. Who else should be solving or sharing this?
  6. When do we stop, shrink, or redesign it?
